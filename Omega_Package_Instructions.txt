- Expanded View: Recursively lists every subfolder and file (.py) within the system.

Example:

├── emotional_core_logic/
│   ├── emotion_broadcast_hub.py
│   ├── emotion_delay_throttle.py

2. ARCHITECTURE
Explains how the folders and files map to system functions.
